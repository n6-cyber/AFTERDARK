AFTERDARK

What AfterDark stands for

01

Purposeful Lighting

We support lighting that protects safety and visibility while reducing glare, waste, and skyglow.

02

Community Awareness

Awareness grows when people understand how light pollution affects health, wildlife, and the night sky.

03

Environmental Respect

We champion policies and behavior that preserve natural darkness for animals, ecosystems, and humans alike.

04

Innovation & Balance

Smart technology should harmonize light and dark, using energy efficiently without compromising the night.

Why these values matter

AfterDark is more than a project — it is a commitment to restore the beauty of the night while preserving the benefits of modern lighting.

  • Protecting star-filled skies reconnects people with nature and science.
  • Reducing energy waste lowers costs and carbon emissions.
  • Improving wellbeing by respecting human circadian rhythms and wildlife patterns.
