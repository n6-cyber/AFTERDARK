AFTERDARK

Goals and the plan that will move us forward

Organizational Goals

01

Increase awareness

Build education campaigns, outreach materials, and workshops to grow public understanding of light pollution and night sky preservation.

02

Promote best practice lighting

Recommend and support low-glare, energy-efficient lighting solutions that balance safety and environmental sensitivity.

03

Strengthen partnerships

Work with municipalities, environmental groups, and communities to align local policy with dark sky-friendly standards.

04

Track progress

Measure light reduction, community engagement, and ecological outcomes to keep the project accountable and adaptive.

Action plan timeline

A

Research & baseline

Collect local light pollution data, identify high-impact zones, and evaluate existing lighting assets.

Phase 1
B

Design solutions

Create guidelines, recommend fixtures, and draft outreach materials for residents and stakeholders.

Phase 2
C

Launch awareness

Activate campaigns, host events, and distribute education content to build momentum and community buy-in.

Phase 3
D

Implement change

Support pilot projects, advocate for policy updates, and help partners adopt lighting improvements.

Phase 4

How we measure success

  • Reduction in skyglow and outdoor light spill.
  • Number of organizations and communities engaged.
  • Adoption of dark sky-friendly lighting practices.
  • Feedback from environmental and astronomy groups.
